Found a bug in Toontown Online? Please describe the behavior you encountered in as much detail as you can and submit it to us using the form below.

Press F9 (or Comand+Shift+3 on a Mac) to capture your screenshot and it will be saved as a .jpg file (or .png on a Mac) in the Toontown folder in your Toontown directory (or on your Mac desktop).
